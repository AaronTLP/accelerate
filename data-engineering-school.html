Data Engineering School

Well-curated and managed data is central to the effective use of AI, in science and elsewhere. How can scientists build the data pipelines they need to accelerate their research with AI?

This course will introduce the foundational principles and methods in data engineering that researchers need to make use of AI. Blending online lectures and practical labs, with a focus on the principles of data science and case studies of their application in science, this Winter School will provide participants with hands-on experience of data science for science.

Further details about content and how to apply to join the course will follow in October 2022.
